About Us

Actiondo International Ent. (AIE) was established to cater the needs of the rubber, paint, friction, transport and logistics, tobacco and similar industries. Presently, it carries the Borgwaldt Instruments and Flavor with factory in Hamburg, Germany, suppliers from India namely Sun Ark Aluminum Industries Ltd., from Indonesia namely PT Dua Kuda Indonesia, Showa Denko from Japan, Cardolite from the USA , India and China and Sutee Tankers and Trucks from Thailand.

AIE is a customer service oriented company. It assures the customers of good quality service.

Some of Our Partners



Showa Denko

The Showa Denko Group continues to create excellent individualized products throught interconnection of inorganic, aluminum, and organic chemical technologies, thereby contributing to society in which affluence and sustainability are harmonized.

Sun Ark Aluminum Industries Ltd.

A name synonymous to quality, Sun Ark Aluminium Industries (P) Limited. Today stands tall in the worldwide market in the area of Aluminium Granules. Sun Ark Aluminium Industries have earned a substantial market base in a very limited span of time.

Cardolite

Cardolite is the world leader in developing, manufacturing, and promoting products using natural and renewable cashew nutshell liquid technology.
